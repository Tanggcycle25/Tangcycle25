My story with the cycling

Do you know the movie Asterix & Obelix?(If not don’t worry!)

The story tells that Obelix fell into the magic potion when he was a baby, right? Well, it's the same thing for me but I fell into cycling world...

Cycling is also my dad's passion.

My godfather

Indeed, I am born exactly the morning my dad was organizing a big cycling race in my city. Normally, I was supposed to be born 20 days later but I think I just wanted to see the race. And this is only the beginning of my story with cycling...

That particular day, one of the greatest cyclists of all time, was at my home, Bernard Hinault

He came to see me a few hours after my birth and I think he transmitted to me this love of cycling. But that's not all, above all, he became my godfather. (Cycling came to me before I could even know it …)

My dad

During the weekends, my dad goes all over France to animate cycling races, in addition to his work.

As soon as I knew how to walk and talk, I went with him and that's how I could start dreaming when I saw in reality the athletes who were on TV:

Jeremy Maison

Mathieu Van der Poel

Dreaming was fine but doing is better for me … It was precisely the problem...

Until I was 13 years old, my parents didn't want to allow me to do cycling in competition... So, I played tennis and football during several years and when I was 13, I began competing.

I quickly got into the competition game and it became a way of life:

  1. School
  2. Bike
  3. Sleep
    4.

And repeat!

First race and first victory

Almost my whole life is about this sport. All my social networks are full of photos, video, news about cycling. My favorite web page is not Facebook but DirectVelo. Also, thanks to the competitions, I could meet great friends with whom I am still in touch even if we don't do any more races and trips together but… All good things have an end.And the end came...but maybe not definitively.

Since I am in post-graduate studies, I have reduced my number of races as I can train less because of the internships and stays abroad.

Now, you know my biggest passion but also a large part of my history up to now. I hope you liked it because other articles to get to know me will be published soon.
